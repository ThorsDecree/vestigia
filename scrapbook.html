📖 The Community Scrapbook

A place where little things are allowed to matter.

Every page here was created by someone spending time in and around VESTIGIA. Some are playful. Some are practical. Some remember difficult things. Some simply celebrate a really good cup of tea.

None of them were assigned. None of them were optimized. They're simply pages people wanted to keep.

Take your time.
